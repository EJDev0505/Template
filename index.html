Discovering the Most Beautiful Beaches in Palawan

Nacpan Beach

Nacpan Beach is widely considered as the most beautiful beach in El Nido, captivating the hearts of visitors with its serene and tropical paradise-like setting. If you're looking to escape from the crowds and soak in some sun, this is the perfect destination. Located approximately 45 minutes away from El Nido town, Nacpan Beach is easily accessible via private tricycle or van. For those seeking accommodations in the area, ANGKLA Beach Club & Boutique Resort comes highly recommended.

Seven Commandos Beach

Seven Commandos Beach is a true paradise on earth, boasting sparkling white sands, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and a stunning backdrop of swaying coconut trees. Widely considered one of the best beaches in El Nido, this idyllic spot is accessible only by boat and is typically the final stop on island-hopping tours. For those looking to make the most of their visit to Seven Commandos Beach, we highly recommend a stay at Vellago Resort. Getting to Seven Commandos Beach is easy with private boat transfers available from El Nido town. Additionally, Seven Commandos Beach is featured in El Nido Tour A, making it a must-visit destination for any trip to El Nido.

Sabang Beach

Nestled along the rugged, untouched coastline of Palawan's eastern shore, Sabang Beach is renowned as one of the island's most popular destinations, serving as the gateway to the famous Underground River. Despite its popularity, Sabang Beach remains a tranquil and serene place to unwind and recharge. For those planning to visit Sabang Beach, Daluyon Beach and Mountain Resort comes highly recommended for an unforgettable stay. Getting to Sabang Beach is relatively easy, with a 2 hour drive from Puerto Princesa airport. Private vans are also available for transportation from El Nido.

Las Cabanas

La Cabanas Beach, located just 15 minutes from El Nido town, has quickly become a favorite destination among travelers. With its beautiful white sand and stunning sunsets, it's the perfect place to unwind and relax. For the best experience, stay at the Maremegmeg Beach Club. To reach La Cabanas Beach, simply take a 15-minute tricycle ride from El Nido town

Palawan Beach is a pristine tropical haven located on Sentosa Island, Singapore. With its powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters, it's a popular destination for relaxation and family fun. The iconic suspension bridge connecting to a small islet adds to its charm, making Palawan Beach a delightful retreat for visitors.
